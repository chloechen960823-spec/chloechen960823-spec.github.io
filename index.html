How I move work forward

From a fuzzy question to a product people can trust.

I use design to make decisions visible. Evidence and constraints stay present as the work moves from framing to a testable product.

01

Frame the real decision

Align the user need, business intent and constraints before polishing a screen.

02

Build the system, not a snapshot

Translate the direction into flows, states and reusable interaction rules.

03

Prototype to learn

Use clickable and coded prototypes to expose gaps, test behaviour and refine the handoff.

Strategy becomes useful only when it survives the interface.
